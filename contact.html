Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right size Carzone Bike Helmet?

Measure the circumference of your head just above your eyebrows and refer to our size chart. Our helmets feature adjustable fit systems for a customized fit.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your Carzone Bike Helmet, you can return it for a full refund.

Do Carzone Bike Helmets come with a warranty?

Yes! All Carzone Bike Helmets come with a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. We also offer a crash replacement program.

How often should I replace my helmet?

We recommend replacing your helmet every 3-5 years, or immediately after any significant impact, even if there's no visible damage.

Are Carzone Bike Helmets safety certified?

Absolutely! All our helmets meet or exceed CPSC, EN 1078, and AS/NZS 2063 safety standards, ensuring world-class protection.

Where can I buy Carzone Bike Helmets?

Our helmets are available through authorized retailers nationwide and online. Contact us for a list of dealers in your area.
